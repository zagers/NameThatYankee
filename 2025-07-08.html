Career Highlights & Facts

  • He was famously featured in a 1996 episode of the sitcom "Seinfeld" when Frank Costanza, George's father, called his trade from the Yankees "a mistake."
  • Known for his bald head, goatee, and powerful throwing arm, he was one of the most recognizable players of his era.
  • He was selected to the American League All-Star team in 1996, the same year he won a Silver Slugger Award.
  • He was inducted into the Seattle Mariners Hall of Fame in 2004, becoming the first player from the memorable 1995 "Refuse to Lose" team to receive the honor.
