Michael Shane Spencer is an American former professional baseball outfielder. He played a total of 538 games in Major League Baseball (MLB) for the New York Yankees, Texas Rangers, Cleveland Indians, and New York Mets, compiling 438 hits, 59 home runs, and 242 runs batted in.
