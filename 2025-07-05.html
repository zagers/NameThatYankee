Daryl Boston

Career Highlights & Facts

  • A former 1st round pick (7th overall) in the 1981 MLB Draft by the Chicago White Sox.
  • He was selected by the Colorado Rockies in the 1992 MLB Expansion Draft, making him an original member of the team.
  • After his playing days, he enjoyed a long coaching career, including serving as the White Sox first base coach from 2013 to 2020.
  • He was traded from the White Sox to the Mets in 1990 in exchange for left-handed pitcher John Candelaria.
