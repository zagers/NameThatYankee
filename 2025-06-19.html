Melido Perez

Career Highlights & Facts

  • Melido Pérez was known for his sinkerball, a pitch that induces ground balls.
  • He played in the 1995 All-Star Game while pitching for the Chicago White Sox.
  • He was originally drafted by the California Angels in the 1983 amateur draft.
  • After his playing career, he served as a pitching coach in the minor leagues and for the Dominican Republic national team.
