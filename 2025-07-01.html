Paul Quantrill

Career Highlights & Facts

  • Paul Quantrill pitched for 7 different Major League Baseball teams over his 14-year career from 1992 to 2005.
  • He led the American League in games pitched for four consecutive seasons (1998-2001) while with the Toronto Blue Jays, and led all of Major League Baseball in games pitched in 2000 (88 games), 2003 (89 games, with the Dodgers), and 2004 (86 games, with the Yankees).
  • Quantrill holds the Major League Baseball record for most relief appearances over a four-year span (320 from 1998-2001) and a five-year span (394 from 1998-2002).
  • Despite his extensive career as a prominent setup reliever, he only recorded 21 career saves, often pitching ahead of Hall of Fame closer Mariano Rivera during his time with the New York Yankees.
