Paul Quantrill

Career Highlights & Facts

  • Paul Quantrill was originally drafted by the Boston Red Sox in the 6th round of the 1987 MLB draft.
  • He was named an American League All-Star in 2001 while pitching for the Toronto Blue Jays, marking his only career All-Star selection.
  • Quantrill was known for his durability and high volume of appearances, leading the American League in appearances three times (1998, 1999, 2001).
  • His son, Cal Quantrill, is also a Major League Baseball pitcher, continuing the family legacy in the sport.
