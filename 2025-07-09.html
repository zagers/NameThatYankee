Jim Beattie

Career Highlights & Facts

  • As a rookie, he won Game 5 of the 1978 World Series for the Yankees against the Los Angeles Dodgers.
  • He was traded from the Yankees to the Mariners in a major 8-player deal after the 1979 season.
  • After his playing career, he became a successful front office executive, serving as General Manager for both the Montreal Expos and Baltimore Orioles.
  • He was originally drafted by the Yankees in the 4th round of the 1975 MLB Draft out of Dartmouth College.
