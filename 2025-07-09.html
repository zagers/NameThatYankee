Jim Beattie

Career Highlights & Facts

  • He was drafted by the New York Yankees in the 4th round of the 1975 Major League Baseball draft.
  • Beattie was a key piece in a significant seven-player trade on November 1, 1979, when the Yankees sent him to the Seattle Mariners along with Ruppert Jones, Rick Anderson, and Juan Beníquez for Willie Horton, Larry Milbourne, and two others.
  • He was a highly regarded student and athlete, graduating from Dartmouth College in 1976 and playing both baseball and basketball there.
  • After his baseball career, he attended medical school and became a successful orthopedic surgeon.
