Wikipedia Summary:
Enrique Wilson Martes is a Dominican former professional baseball player. In his eight-season Major League Baseball career, Wilson played with the Cleveland Indians (1997–2000), Pittsburgh Pirates (2000–01), New York Yankees (2001–04), and Chicago Cubs (2005) as a utility infielder. He batted switch and threw right-handed.
