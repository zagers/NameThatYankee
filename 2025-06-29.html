Steve Sax

Career Highlights & Facts

  • He was the National League Rookie of the Year in 1982.
  • He was a five-time MLB All-Star.
  • He won two World Series championships with the Los Angeles Dodgers (1981, 1988).
  • During the late 1980s, he famously suffered from a temporary inability to make routine throws to first base, sometimes referred to as 'Steve Sax Syndrome'.
