Steve Sax

Career Highlights & Facts

  • Steve Sax was named the National League Rookie of the Year in 1982.
  • He was a five-time MLB All-Star (1982, 1983, 1986, 1989, 1990).
  • He was a member of two World Series championship teams with the Los Angeles Dodgers (1981, 1988).
  • During his career, he famously battled 'the yips,' a psychological condition causing difficulty with routine throws, particularly to first base.
