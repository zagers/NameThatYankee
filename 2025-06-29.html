Steve Sax

Career Highlights & Facts

  • Steve Sax was a five-time MLB All-Star (1982, 1983, 1986, 1989, 1993).
  • He won two World Series championships with the Los Angeles Dodgers in 1981 and 1988.
  • He finished second in the National League Rookie of the Year voting in 1982.
  • Sax famously overcame a period in 1983 where he struggled with throwing accuracy, a phenomenon often referred to as 'the yips'.
