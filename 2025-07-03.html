Lyle Overbay

Career Highlights & Facts

  • Lyle Overbay was drafted by the Arizona Diamondbacks in the 18th round of the 1999 MLB draft.
  • Primarily a first baseman, he was highly regarded for his defensive skills, particularly his ability to dig low throws out of the dirt.
  • In 2006, while playing for the Toronto Blue Jays, Overbay led all American League first basemen in both putouts and assists.
  • After concluding his playing career, Overbay returned to the Milwaukee Brewers organization as a special assistant to the general manager.
