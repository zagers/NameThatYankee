Lyle Overbay

Career Highlights & Facts

  • Lyle Overbay led the National League in triples (12) during his breakout 2004 season with the Milwaukee Brewers.
  • He had a unique career path, playing for seven different MLB franchises (Diamondbacks, Brewers, Blue Jays, Pirates, Braves, Yankees) and having two separate stints with the Milwaukee Brewers.
  • Known for his consistent hitting, Overbay maintained a career batting average of .266 over 14 Major League seasons.
  • He was part of the New York Yankees in 2013, playing 147 games and hitting 14 home runs as their primary first baseman.
