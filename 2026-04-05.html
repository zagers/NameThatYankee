Retrosheet


Austin Thomas Slater is an American professional baseball outfielder for the New York Mets organization. He has previously played in MLB for the San Francisco Giants, Cincinnati Reds, Baltimore Orioles, Chicago White Sox, New York Yankees, and Miami Marlins. He played college baseball for the Stanford Cardinal, and was selected by the Giants in the eighth round of the 2014 MLB draft. He made his MLB debut in 2017 with the Giants.
