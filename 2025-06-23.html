Paul O'Neill "The Warrior"

Career Highlights & Facts

  • He won the American League batting title in 1994 with a .359 average.
  • O'Neill was a five-time MLB All-Star (1991, 1994, 1995, 1997, 1998).
  • He won five World Series championships during his career: one with the Cincinnati Reds in 1990 and four with the New York Yankees in 1996, 1998, 1999, and 2000.
  • He played in three perfect games: Tom Browning's in 1988, David Wells' in 1998, and David Cone's in 1999.
