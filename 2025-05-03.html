Wikipedia Summary:
Reid Michael Brignac is an American former professional baseball infielder and current coach. He played in Major League Baseball (MLB) for the Tampa Bay Rays, Colorado Rockies, New York Yankees, Philadelphia Phillies, Miami Marlins, and Atlanta Braves. He is currently serves as the manager for the Binghamton Rumble Ponies.
