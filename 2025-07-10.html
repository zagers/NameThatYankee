Luis Sojo

Career Highlights & Facts

  • Luis Sojo won four World Series championships with the New York Yankees (1996, 1998, 1999, 2000).
  • Known for his defensive versatility, he played every infield position (first base, second base, third base, and shortstop) during his Major League career.
  • He had two separate tenures with the New York Yankees, including being traded back to them mid-season in 2000 after starting the year with the Pittsburgh Pirates.
  • His final Major League hit was a dramatic walk-off single in the bottom of the 12th inning of Game 5 of the 2001 World Series against the Arizona Diamondbacks.
