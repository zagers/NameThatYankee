Jesse Barfield

Career Highlights & Facts

  • Jesse Barfield was widely acclaimed for his exceptional defensive play in right field, earning him two Gold Glove Awards in 1986 and 1987, largely due to his powerful and accurate throwing arm.
  • He holds the distinction of being the first player in Toronto Blue Jays franchise history to hit 30 home runs in a single season (1986) and also the first to reach 200 career home runs for the club.
  • His trade from the Toronto Blue Jays to the New York Yankees on April 30, 1989, for Al Leiter was a significant one-for-one exchange of prominent players.
  • On April 15, 1990, Barfield hit the 10,000th home run in the illustrious history of the New York Yankees franchise.
