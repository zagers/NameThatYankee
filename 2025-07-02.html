Jesse Barfield

Career Highlights & Facts

  • Jesse Barfield won a Gold Glove Award in 1989, a season in which he was traded from the Toronto Blue Jays to the New York Yankees.
  • He was the first player in Toronto Blue Jays history to hit 30 home runs in a single season, achieving this in 1985.
  • In 1986, Barfield led the American League with 40 home runs.
  • He was part of the Blue Jays teams that won back-to-back American League East titles in 1985 and 1989.
