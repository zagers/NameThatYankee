Career Highlights & Facts

  • Jesse Barfield won an American League Gold Glove Award for his defensive excellence in right field in 1986.

  • Barfield was renowned for possessing one of the strongest and most accurate throwing arms among outfielders of his era.
  • He was originally drafted by the Toronto Blue Jays in the 9th round of the 1977 Major League Baseball Draft.
