Wikipedia Summary:
Todd Anthony Greene is an American former professional baseball catcher. In an 11-year career, he played for the Anaheim Angels (1996–1999), Toronto Blue Jays (2000), New York Yankees (2001), Texas Rangers (2002–2003), Colorado Rockies (2004–2005), and San Francisco Giants (2006) of Major League Baseball (MLB). He batted and threw right-handed.
