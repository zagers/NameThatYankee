Rick "Big Daddy" Reuschel

Career Highlights & Facts

  • On August 21, 1975, he and his brother Paul became the first and only siblings to combine on a shutout.
  • He finished 3rd in the National League Cy Young Award voting twice, twelve years apart (in 1977 and 1989).
  • Winner of the 1985 Hutch Award, given annually to a player who best exemplifies a fighting spirit and competitive desire.
  • Was a three-time All-Star and a two-time Gold Glove Award winner for his excellent fielding as a pitcher.
