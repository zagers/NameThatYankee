Rick Reuschel "Big Daddy"

Career Highlights & Facts

  • Rick Reuschel was a two-time MLB All-Star, first in 1977 with the Chicago Cubs and again in 1987 with the San Francisco Giants.
  • He won the National League Comeback Player of the Year award in 1987 after overcoming a rotator cuff injury.
  • Reuschel famously started Game 3 of the 1989 World Series for the San Francisco Giants, which was interrupted by the Loma Prieta earthquake.
  • Known for his longevity and stout physique, he pitched for 20 seasons in Major League Baseball.
