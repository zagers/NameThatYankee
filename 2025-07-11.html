Rick Reuschel "Big Daddy"

Career Highlights & Facts

  • Rick Reuschel was one of only a handful of pitchers to hit 10 or more career home runs.
  • He was the oldest pitcher in MLB history to win 17 games in a season, achieving this at age 40 in 1989.
  • Reuschel was traded twice during the 1981 season, first from the Cubs to the Yankees and then from the Yankees to the Pirates.
  • His two All-Star selections were separated by a decade, occurring in 1977 and 1987.
