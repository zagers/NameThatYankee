Darryl Strawberry "Straw"

Career Highlights & Facts

  • He was the first overall pick in the 1980 MLB Draft by the New York Mets.
  • He won the National League Rookie of the Year award in 1983.
  • Strawberry is one of only five players in MLB history to hit 300 home runs and steal 200 bases.
  • He won four World Series championships: one with the New York Mets (1986) and three with the New York Yankees (1996, 1998, 1999).
